Why GISTDA-OpenAI?

GISTDA-OpenAI is a research initiative focused on developing Thailand-specific geospatial foundation models capable of understanding remote sensing imagery, land-use dynamics, environmental monitoring, and multi-modal Earth observation data at national scale.

🛰️

Earth Observation Intelligence

Leveraging Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, DEM, NDVI, SAR, and geospatial datasets for advanced AI-driven mapping.

🌏

Thailand Foundation Models

Developing geospatial AI systems specialized for Thailand’s environmental, agricultural, urban, and disaster domains.

🧠

Foundation AI Research

Combining generative AI, diffusion models, geospatial reasoning, and foundation model adaptation.

⚡

National-scale Deployment

Designed for scalable deployment on cloud GPU infrastructure, supporting future nationwide geospatial intelligence systems.

GISTDA TerraMind Thailand-Tiny

Built upon TerraMind foundation model research, the Thailand-Tiny Beta release demonstrates geospatial generative AI for land-use and land-cover understanding using remote sensing imagery.

📡

Satellite Modalities

Supports Sentinel-2 RGB, Sentinel-2 L2A, Sentinel-1 SAR, NDVI, DEM, and multi-source Earth observation inputs.

🧩

Cross-Modal Generation

Generate land-use maps and remote sensing outputs directly from satellite imagery using diffusion-based models.

🏆

Research-grade Infrastructure

Powered by TerraTorch, HuggingFace ecosystem, PyTorch, and modern geospatial AI pipelines.

🚀

Future Thailand Fine-tuning

Future releases aim to specialize the model for Thai agriculture, urban expansion, flooding, and environmental intelligence.

Research Vision

The long-term vision of GISTDA-OpenAI is to establish a national geospatial AI ecosystem capable of supporting Thailand’s strategic Earth observation missions, disaster monitoring, agriculture, urban intelligence, environmental sustainability, and future AI-native geospatial systems.

🌾

Agriculture Intelligence

AI models for crop monitoring, rice mapping, drought analysis, and precision agriculture.

🌊

Disaster Monitoring

Flood prediction, wildfire detection, environmental change analysis, and rapid response mapping.

🏙️

Urban Geospatial AI

National urban analytics for smart cities, land-use expansion, and infrastructure planning.

🤖

Geo Foundation Models

Towards large-scale geospatial reasoning models trained specifically for Southeast Asia and Thailand.
